Amboseli
National Park

The savannah grassland ecosystem spreads across the Kenya-Tanzania border. Amboseli is the best place in Africa to get close to over 1,600 free-ranging elephants, with the snowcapped summit of Mt. Kilimanjaro forming the backdrop for unforgettable photographs.

Drive from Nairobi

250 km · ~4 hrs

Key Wildlife

Elephants, Lions, Cheetah

Highlight

Mt. Kilimanjaro Views

Lake
Naivasha

One of Africa's most renowned freshwater lakes. A boat safari lets you spot hippos and hundreds of bird species. The shores host giraffe, zebra, waterbuck, and eland. Cap the afternoon with a walking safari at the peninsula-shaped Crescent Island — Naivasha's best-kept secret.

Drive from Amboseli

350 km · ~6 hrs

Key Activity

Hippo Boat Ride

Hidden Gem

Crescent Island Walk

Lake Nakuru
National Park

Famed for its extraordinary flamingo displays — sometimes millions at once — Lake Nakuru is also one of the few sanctuaries in Africa hosting both black and white rhinos. The endangered Rothschild's giraffe and the scenic Makalia Waterfall add to its remarkable appeal.

Drive from Naivasha

100 km · ~1 hr

Key Wildlife

Flamingos, Rhinos, Giraffe

Special Occasion

New Year's Eve dinner

Masai
Mara

The crown jewel of every Kenyan safari. The Masai Mara holds the highest concentration of wildlife in Kenya. All the Big Five roam here freely. Big cats — lion, leopard, cheetah — are spotted regularly. Optional hot air balloon rides at sunrise offer a perspective few ever experience.

Drive from Nakuru

300 km · ~5 hrs

Key Wildlife

Big Five + Big Cats

Optional Add-on

Hot Air Balloon USD 450

Nairobi
National Park

The world's only national park located within a major capital city. Lions, leopards, cheetahs, buffaloes, giraffes, zebras, and over 400 bird species roam freely just minutes from Nairobi's skyscrapers. The iconic juxtaposition of raw wilderness against a modern skyline makes this a truly one-of-a-kind wildlife experience.

Distance from CBD

7 km · ~20 mins

Key Wildlife

Lions, Rhinos, Giraffe

Unique Feature

City Skyline Backdrop

Volcanoes National Park
Rwanda

Home to the endangered mountain gorilla — one of the rarest and most awe-inspiring animals on earth. Nestled in the misty Virunga Mountains of northern Rwanda, Volcanoes National Park offers an intimate gorilla trekking experience unlike anything else on the African continent.

Country

Rwanda

Key Experience

Mountain Gorilla Trek

Best Season

Jun – Sep · Dec – Feb

Serengeti
National Park

Tanzania's legendary Serengeti is the stage for nature's greatest spectacle — the Great Wildebeest Migration, where over 1.5 million animals thunder across endless golden plains. Beyond the migration, the Serengeti delivers year-round excellence: dense lion prides, elusive leopards in acacia trees, massive elephant families, and skies painted with countless birds.

Country

Tanzania

Key Wildlife

Big Five · Great Migration

Park Size

14,750 sq km

Bali
Indonesia

The Island of the Gods captivates with its harmony of ancient Hindu culture, emerald rice terraces, surf-kissed beaches, and lush jungle retreats. From the spiritual stillness of Ubud's temples to the electric sunsets of Seminyak, Bali is endlessly layered. We craft personalised Bali escapes that go far beyond the tourist trail.

Indonesia

Best For

Dubai
UAE

Where the impossible becomes the everyday. Dubai is a dazzling collision of futuristic architecture, world-class shopping, desert adventure, and Michelin-star dining. Ascend the Burj Khalifa, glide across the Dubai Frame, race dunes in a 4×4, then end the evening at a rooftop bar overlooking an illuminated skyline.

Country

Malindi
Kenya Coast

Kenya's best-kept coastal secret. Malindi blends Swahili heritage, pristine white-sand beaches, and some of East Africa's finest coral reefs into a relaxed, unhurried paradise. Snorkel or dive the Malindi Marine National Park, explore the ancient ruins at Gede, or simply let the warm Indian Ocean carry your cares away.

Distance from Nairobi

~1 hr by Air

Best For

Beach, Diving, Swahili Culture

Best Season

Oct – Mar

Malaysia

Country

Malaysia
